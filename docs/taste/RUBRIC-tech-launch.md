# Tech Launch / Product Rubric (v1.0, 2026-07-18)

This rubric evaluates the "Tech Launch / Product" genre (e.g., product introductions, reviews, and studio hardware walkthroughs). Scoring is on a 1–5 scale per criterion.

---

## Rubric Breakdown

### G-TL-1: Hook Macro & Central Question (Weight: 20%)
* **Description:** Visual opening starting with high-quality close-ups or macro shots of the product, paired with a scripted central question or thesis statement.
* **Zing Measures:**
  * Zoom and macro frame changes in the first 0–3s.
  * Time-to-proposition (target: under 3s, thesis statement spoken immediately).
* **AI Judges:**
  * Thesis clarity: does the hook set up a clear tension or specific question?
* **Confidence Tier:** T1 (YouTube Playbook attention-grab) | T3 (Practitioner teardowns).
* **Source Trace:**
  * Framework: H1, H2.
  * Teardown: Marques Brownlee (macro shot and scripted central tension), Dave2D (direct device close-up).

### G-TL-2: Invisible & Subtractive Editing (Weight: 15%)
* **Description:** Visual storytelling that removes unnecessary graphics, transitions, or flash. Cuts should keep the focus entirely on the product design and utility.
* **Zing Measures:**
  * Cut interval variability.
  * Absence of standard template transitions (flashes, spins).
* **AI Judges:**
  * Subtractive score: does every shot serve the narrative and explain the hardware, or are there "darling" shots that add no value?
* **Confidence Tier:** T3 (Walter Murch's Rule of Six / Apple Keynote / MKBHD teardown).
* **Source Trace:**
  * Framework: E1, E2.
  * Teardown: Marques Brownlee ("killing your darlings" subtractive storytelling), Apple Event (seamless transitions).

### G-TL-3: Tactile Sound Design & Audio Purity (Weight: 15%)
* **Description:** High-clarity vocal tracking combined with high-quality tactile sound design (foley) that captures hardware physical qualities (clicks, hinges, fans).
* **Zing Measures:**
  * High-frequency transient spikes matching video actions (pressing buttons, opening lids).
  * Speech-to-noise ratio in dialogue sections.
* **AI Judges:**
  * Audio quality: evaluates voice recording clarity and the realism of hardware sound effects.
* **Confidence Tier:** T1 (TikTok sound/music present) | T3 (Practitioner-consensus).
* **Source Trace:**
  * Framework: P1.
  * Teardown: Dave2D (tactile keyboard and fan noise), Marques Brownlee (crisp studio voice track).

### G-TL-4: Macro Visual Quality & Studio Lighting (Weight: 15%)
* **Description:** Professional composition, controlled lighting, and high contrast that highlight device build quality and textures.
* **Zing Measures:**
  * Resolution (>=1080p target), color bit-depth, aspect ratio.
  * Frame exposure consistency.
* **AI Judges:**
  * Lighting quality: evaluates visual texture clarity (clean studio/pastel desktop vs low-light noise).
* **Confidence Tier:** T1 (TikTok vertical requirements) | T3 (Practitioner-consensus).
* **Source Trace:**
  * Framework: P1.
  * Teardown: Marques Brownlee (8K macro RED shots), Dave2D (pastel flat table framing).

### G-TL-5: Technical Information Density & Graphic Matting (Weight: 10%)
* **Description:** Clear presentation of specifications (CPU, RAM, benchmarks) using clean, minimalist overlays, avoiding large text blocks or cluttered backgrounds.
* **Zing Measures:**
  * Screen space area occupied by text cards.
  * Match between text display duration and average reading time.
* **AI Judges:**
  * Graphic styling: evaluations of spec graphic readability and layout integration.
* **Confidence Tier:** T1 (Instagram downranking text-heavy content) | T3 (Practitioner-consensus).
* **Source Trace:**
  * Framework: P2.
  * Teardown: Marques Brownlee (grey/white spec overlays), Dave2D (thin pastel-themed tables).

### G-TL-6: Narrative Structure & Value Verdict (Weight: 10%)
* **Description:** Standard progression from industrial design to internals/performance, camera/battery, concluding with an honest price-value judgment.
* **Zing Measures:**
  * Video duration and section divisions.
* **AI Judges:**
  * Structure: checks for a logical, complete review progression and the presence of an uncompromised price-value verdict.
* **Confidence Tier:** T3 (Practitioner-consensus).
* **Source Trace:**
  * Teardown: Marques Brownlee (design -> performance -> camera -> verdict), Dave2D (design -> performance -> internals -> verdict).

### G-TL-7: Original Perspective & Anti-Slop (Weight: 15%)
* **Description:** The review must commit to a clear stance regarding the product's quality, avoiding manufacturer copy or templated bullet points.
* **Zing Measures:**
  * Transcript similarity against manufacturer press release text.
* **AI Judges:**
  * Stance commitment: does the review offer a clear opinion or does it both-side every claim?
* **Confidence Tier:** T1 (YouTube monetization policies) | T3 (Practitioner-consensus).
* **Source Trace:**
  * Framework: A1, A2.
  * Teardown: Dave2D (hype-free conversations), Marques Brownlee ("phone you shouldn't buy" critique).
