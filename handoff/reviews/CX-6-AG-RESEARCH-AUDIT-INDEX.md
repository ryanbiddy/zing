  Claude Desktop's review UI. DR-3's config rewrite, backup, forced restart,
  conversion percentages, and timing numbers are not supported.
- Google says structured data can make pages eligible for rich results but
  does not guarantee display or ranking; its AI-search guidance requires no
  special schema. DR-4's GEO priority claim and its growth receipts do not
  survive.
- Readwise documents offline operation, export/API paths, and an MCP
  connection. DR-7's blanket competitor-gap premise is false.
- Apple's hardened-runtime exceptions must be justified by an actual failing
  signed-bundle test. DR-6 treats security-sensitive entitlements as generic
  Python dependency requirements without that proof.
- Uoink's current canonical brand files disagree with DR-5's exact
  vermillion and acid values. Planned sibling marks and tray assets are
  proposals, not verified assets.

The audit narrows every dossier to source-backed carry-forward, routes a
specific redo or quarantine for every failed claim set, and identifies
downstream documents contaminated by DR-2, DR-4, and DR-7.
