### Honest coverage numbers

- **Every banded stat is n=3 of 3** — the minimum the sprint gate
  allows, and it shows: bands are wide because the set is
  heterogeneous (a 32s fast-cut short next to a 100s conversational
  interview).
- **Judged half: empty.** All 3 sources are UNJUDGED — no `study`
  judgments were run on them, so this is a measured-only profile.
  `judged: {}`, `unjudged_source_slugs` = all three. That is exactly
  the "honest on mixed judged/unjudged sets" case the S2 spec demands,
  at its extreme.
- **music_present_rate 1.0 is built on n=1** — only the rapid-fire
  interview had a conclusive music measurement; the other two are
  wall-to-wall speech with no gaps to measure a bed in.
- **Caption stats are pooled point values, not bands** — and the
  ALL-CAPS rate (43%) pools a genuinely split convention: per-source
  0.98 / 0.03 / 0.42. The profile does not agree with itself on
  caption case.
- **Transition counts come from the opt-in synthetic-calibrated
  detector** (run on all 3 sources). 29 dissolves across three
  talking-head videos is plausibly detector over-calling on
  animation/motion — treat kind ratios as weak evidence.

(median 73s). Where the profile is honest about not knowing Ryan's
taste yet: caption case (split), music (n=1), and pacing spread
(the fast pole cuts 4.7× as often as the slow pole).
