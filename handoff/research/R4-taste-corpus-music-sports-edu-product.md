## Deeper Threads

1. **How do sports-reaction cuts differ from tech review cuts?** sports-reaction timing is driven by audio excitement cues (e.g., screaming, ball impacts), whereas tech review cuts are driven by visual continuity rules (matching spoken specs to the device macro view).
2. **What are the constraints of audio-first hooks in music videos?** Music hooks rely on instant audio quality (high-fidelity chords or crowd harmony). If the initial vocal track has background noise or room reflection, retention drops. How can a local processor identify and tag these high-fidelity audio markers?
3. **How does educational narrative structure affect retention?** Creators like Veritasium use a mystery arc (question -> clues -> resolution), while Mark Rober uses a project build arc (plan -> build -> test). What metadata flags are required to track these structural differences in a local video corpus?
4. **What is the optimal cut-to-beat sync ratio in music production videos?** Andrew Huang cuts precisely on transients. This can feel robotic if sustained. How can an automated editing director introduce random delays to mimic human editorial rhythm?
